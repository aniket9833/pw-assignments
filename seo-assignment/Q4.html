Semantic HTML

Introduction

Semantic tags in HTML are elements that carry meaning about the structure and content of a web page. They provide information to both browsers and developers, making the HTML code more readable, maintainable, and accessible. Semantic tags help define the purpose of different sections of a webpage, and they play a crucial role in search engine optimization (SEO) by conveying the hierarchy and significance of content.
